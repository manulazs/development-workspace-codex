# Code Of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and precise.
- Keep feedback technical, actionable, and evidence-based.
- Assume contributors are trying to improve the workspace.
- Disagree by explaining tradeoffs and risks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, credentials, or sensitive operational details.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Bypassing documented safety or validation rules.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove contributions that violate this code of conduct or put users at risk. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocked participation.

## Reporting

Report issues to the repository maintainer. Do not include secrets or private data in public reports.
